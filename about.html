About

Alphabet of Refusal is a community-driven project where anyone is invited to pick a keyword and reflect, abstractly or tangibly, on the different ways we encounter, respond, and contend with refusal. The project hopes to explore how we can reject the current social, cultural, political, and economic structures imposed on us, using art as a tool to navigate this current landscape. Keywords related to refusal can be tangible, like “homeland,” or more abstract, “presence.”
